Welcome to Safe Space

SAFE SPACE is a feature-rich platform for nuanced conversations, confidential reportage of abuse that will help cater for the victims of abuse while taking their mental health into consideration. We aim at raising awareness, mobilizing campaigns and driving action towards improving individual response to all patterns of violence.

On SAFE SPACE, you can speak your mind, ventilate your grievances, report abuse etc. A platform that engages professionals such as doctors, psychologists, human rights activists as well as Legal practitioners. We aim at building a just society devoid of all forms of gender-based violence/sexual abuse capable of undermining the existence of girls and women in all forms and also to cater for the society's mental health in general and make sure justice is served accordingly
